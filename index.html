Share

Use our mobile App or website to request a food pick-up when your business or organization has leftovers.

Transport

Our algorithm instantly matches your excess food to a nearby recipient agency (like a homeless shelter), based on their needs, as well as an on-demand driver who picks up and delivers the food. All parties are notified and updated in real-time. Individuals can also deliver food from one place to another


Request

Agency requests for food based on their needs. This is in case someone cannot transport but has needs.

Solution

We bridge the gap between these issues by connecting people and ensuring that the untouched left over food, can be distributed to people in need of food.Our platform uses technology to streamline the process of food recovery and distribution, giving businesses and organizations the power to fight hunger and food waste within minutes.
